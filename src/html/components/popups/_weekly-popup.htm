Weekly Cashback

The Weekly Cashback is based on your recent activity on Coins.Game and includes factors like your recent P&L, which includes lossback. You can receive a Weekly Cashback every 7 days.

When you activate the Weekly Cashback, you also get Rakeboost. For a certain period of time your rakeback will be increased (Rakeboosts don’t stack).

How to claim your Weekly Cashback?

Go to the Bonuses page and find the Weekly Cashback block. There you can find a countdown timer that indicates the time until the next bonus. If it is available, press the ‘Claim’ button to receive it.
